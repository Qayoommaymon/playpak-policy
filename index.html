PlayPak Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Video Player for Android

PlayPak is a local video player for Android phones and Android TV. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data PlayPak accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

PlayPak does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

The app stores the following locally on your device using SharedPreferences and local cache files:

  • Watch history & playback positions — so you can resume where you left off
  • App settings — theme preference, gesture settings, playback speed
  • Bookmarks & playlists — your saved favorites and custom playlists
  • Thumbnail cache — generated video thumbnails for faster browsing

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from the app settings or by clearing app data in Android Settings.

2. Permissions

PlayPak requests the following Android permissions. Each is required for core functionality:

  • Storage (READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) — To scan, browse, and play video files on your device. Also used to read WhatsApp status files and save them to your Downloads folder.
  • Internet (INTERNET) — To play network video streams (HTTP, HLS, RTSP) and download video files from URLs you provide.
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK) — To keep the screen on during video playback.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE) — For background audio playback and download progress notifications.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — To show playback controls and download progress in the notification shade.

PlayPak does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or any other sensitive data.

3. Network Usage

PlayPak connects to the internet only when you explicitly request it:

  • Playing a network video URL you entered
  • Downloading a video file from a URL you provided

PlayPak does not contain analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any form of telemetry. No data is sent to our servers or any third party.

4. WhatsApp Status Saver

The WhatsApp Status Saver feature reads status files that WhatsApp has already downloaded to your device. PlayPak:

  • Reads files from WhatsApp's local status directories on your device
  • Copies files you choose to save into your Downloads/PlayPak/Statuses folder
  • Does not access WhatsApp accounts, messages, or contacts
  • Does not connect to WhatsApp servers

5. Third-Party Libraries

PlayPak uses open-source libraries for video playback (media_kit/libmpv), thumbnail generation, and audio service notifications. These libraries operate locally on your device and do not transmit data externally.

6. Children's Privacy

PlayPak is a general-purpose video player. It does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children. The app plays video files already present on the user's device.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing watch history and thumbnail cache from the app settings
  • Clearing app data from Android Settings > Apps > PlayPak > Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of PlayPak after changes constitutes acceptance.
